CDSS

Download pdf

This product is specifically designed for Indian Army for Tracking the radio sets. CDSS stands for Combat Decision supporting system. This has got inbuilt GPS and is used to transmit the location data through STARSV MKII, CNR and HX radio sets which are carried by Radio men for communicating. A PC installed with the SIT AWARENESS SYSTEM Software and connected to STARS V MK II/CNR/HX Radiosets will receive the location data from all the CDSS modules and displayed on the maps.
