USB to RS-232/485

Download pdf

We are known as a prestigious source of USB Converter. Provided converter is manufactured under the direction of our expert engineers following the set universal quality standards. Offered FTDI chip based Industry standard USB to RS232 /RS485 converter is extremely used for connecting de-vices or instruments to Windows/Linux based computers over RS232 or RS485 bus. It is available at cost effective price.

Features:
  • FTDI chip.
  • Slide switch for RS232 or RS485 selection
  • USB powered and with cable length of 1 meter
  • Data Transmit and Receive Led Indications
  • Maximum bus protection for RS232 and RS485 lines
  • Adds Virtual COM port in computer when drivers are installed
